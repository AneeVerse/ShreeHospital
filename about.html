About us

Welcome to Shree hospital

This premier super multi-specialty tertiary care hospital in Navi Mumbai is known for its excellence in healthcare. With state-of-the-art facilities, top medical expertise, research, and education, it serves patients locally and beyond. Founded on the principle of "Let all be Blissful, Let all stay Healthy," it's committed to providing quality and compassionate care to every patient. Established in 2000 with 15 beds and 10 doctors, we has grown significantly since then.
